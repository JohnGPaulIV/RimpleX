RimpleX Help Documentation

Entering numbers into the calculator:

Numbers can be entered by either clicking the number on the screen or pressing the corresponding number on the keyboard.

Entering operations into the calculator:

Operations can be entered by either clicking the icon on the screen or pressing certain keys on the keyboard.

Legend:

  1. Change Sign - - [keypress to be entered later] - Changes the sign of the current operand.
  2. Clear - C - [keypress to be entered later] - Clears the current operand.
  3. Backspace - - [keypress to be entered later] - Deletes the most recent character in the operand.
  4. Add - + - [keypress to be entered later] - Starts addition operation.
  5. Subtract - - [keypress to be entered later] - Starts subtraction operation.
  6. Multiply - - [keypress to be entered later] - Starts Multiplication operation.
  7. Divide - - [keypress to be entered later] - Starts Division operation.
  8. Reset - R - [keypress to be entered later] - Clears both the current operand and the previous calculation/equation.
  9. Inverse - Inv - [keypress to be entered later] - Inverts the current operand.
  10. Parentheses - (/) - [keypress to be entered later] - Inserts parentheses into the operand.
  11. Decimal - . - [keypress to be entered later] - Inserts a decimal point into the operand.
  12. Imaginary Part - Imaginary Part - [keypress to be entered later] - Shows the imaginary part of an equation.
  13. Real Part - Real Part - [keypress to be entered later] - Shows the real part of an equation.
  14. Polar Form - Polar Form - [keypress to be entered later] - Shows the polar form of an equation.
  15. Conjugate - z - [keypress to be entered later] - Shows the conjugate of an equation.
  16. Exponent - ^ - [keypress to be entered later] - Inserts an exponent into the operand.
  17. Square root - - [keypress to be entered later] - Inserts a square root operation into the operand.
  18. Logarithm - Log - [keypress to be entered later] - Inserts a logarithm operation into the operand.

Running a calculation:

Press the Equals "=" key on the keyboard or screen to compute the current equation on the left side of the display.
